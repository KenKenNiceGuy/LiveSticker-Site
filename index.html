How can we help?

LiveSticker turns Live Photos into animated stickers directly on your iPhone. For questions, bug reports, or feedback, contact us by email.

Email support Privacy Policy

Before contacting us

Please include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • The LiveSticker version shown on the About screen.
  • A short description of what you expected and what happened.

Please do not email private photos unless they are necessary to explain the issue and you are comfortable sharing them.

支持与反馈

LiveSticker（实况表情包）会直接在 iPhone 上把实况照片处理为动态表情包。 如有问题、错误报告或建议，请通过邮件联系我们。

邮件中建议注明 iPhone 型号、iOS 版本、App 版本以及问题出现的步骤。 除非确有必要且你愿意分享，请不要发送私人照片。
