Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

LiveSticker is designed to process your Live Photos on your device. We do not operate a service that receives or stores your photos, videos, text overlays, or exported stickers.

Information we collect

LiveSticker does not require an account and does not collect personal information through its own servers. The current version does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Photos and media

When you choose a Live Photo, the app accesses only the media you select or authorize. The photo and its paired video are processed locally to create animation frames and GIF files. They are not uploaded to us.

Local storage

Sticker projects, preview images, extracted frames, and app preferences may be stored in the app's local container so you can reopen or share your work. This data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, remove the app, or erase the device data.

Saving and sharing

When you request an export, LiveSticker may save the generated GIF to your photo library or copy it to the system clipboard. The app may also open a third-party messaging app using its registered URL. Any third-party app you choose is governed by its own privacy policy.

System diagnostics

Apple may provide developers with aggregated App Store and diagnostic information when users allow Apple to share it. This information is managed by Apple and does not include the contents of your selected photos or text overlays.

Children's privacy

LiveSticker does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not require an account or upload user content to us, we do not maintain user profiles.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when app features or data practices change. The effective date above will be updated when a revised policy is published.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to livesticker.support@gmail.com.

隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 6 日

LiveSticker（实况表情包）以设备本地处理为核心。我们不运营用于接收或存储用户照片、 视频、文字内容或导出表情包的服务器。

我们收集的信息

LiveSticker 无需注册账号，也不会通过自有服务器收集个人信息。当前版本不包含第三方广告 或数据分析 SDK。

照片与媒体

当你选择实况照片时，App 只访问你选择或授权的媒体。照片及其配对视频仅在设备本地用于提取帧 和生成 GIF，不会上传给我们。

本地存储

为方便再次查看和分享，App 可能在本地保存表情包工程、预览图、提取帧和偏好设置。 这些数据会保留在设备中，直到你在 App 内删除、卸载 App 或清除设备数据。

保存与分享

当你主动导出时，LiveSticker 可能把生成的 GIF 保存到系统相册或写入系统剪贴板， 也可能通过 URL 调起你选择的第三方聊天应用。第三方应用的数据处理遵循其自身隐私政策。

系统诊断信息

如果用户允许 Apple 分享分析数据，Apple 可能向开发者提供汇总的 App Store 与诊断信息。 这些信息由 Apple 管理，不包含你所选择的照片或输入的文字内容。

儿童隐私

LiveSticker 不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。App 无需账号，也不会向我们上传用户内容， 因此我们不会建立用户档案。

政策变更

当 App 功能或数据处理方式发生变化时，我们可能更新本政策，并同步修改页面顶部的生效日期。

联系我们

如对本政策有疑问，请发送邮件至 livesticker.support@gmail.com。
